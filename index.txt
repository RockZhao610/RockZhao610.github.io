However, their online implementation remains largely unexplored due to practical challenges. In this study, we propose an online compound limb-MI-BCI walking system based on a deep learning model and a shared control strategy. We first evaluate three common deep learning models (EEGNet, DeepConvNet, and ShallowConvNet) for decoding performance without complex preprocessing or handcrafted features. Offline results from 11 participants show that all models achieve accuracies significantly above chance, with EEGNet achieving the highest accuracy (70.76 ¬± 8.50%).
